ABOUT SUZUKI
Anybody can drive. To ride, you need passion.
It’s that favorite road where you know the pavement like a good friend. The wild stretch of woods that begs you to wander and explore. Or the call of the racetrack that has you up early on the weekends. At Suzuki, we build motorcycles and ATVs because we share your same enthusiasm to get out and go. For us, it’s a Way of Life.

ATV riders will dig the tough and reliable KingQuad family. Why? Because it’s not just an ATV, it’s a KingQuad ATV. Sportbike riders will be thrilled to climb aboard The King of Sportbikes, the iconic GSX-R1000, while those longing for the ultimate sportbike can swing a leg over the legendary Hayabusa. For fun and adventure on any type of road, hop on a Suzuki DualSport or V-Strom. Families can enjoy the outdoors together riding our youth-oriented off-road motorcycles and ATVs.

We know you share the same passion for powersports as we do at Suzuki. Whether you are an experienced riding enthusiast or a beginning rider, we're certain you'll find something exciting and new about Suzuki products. With a broad range of motorcycles and ATVs in our lineup, we're confident that there’s a Suzuki for you!
Our Commitment To You
